Child Marriage: Causes and Consequences

Description: Child marriage is a global issue that affects countless young girls. In this module, we explore what child marriage is and
its root causes.Child marriage often results in the discontinuation of education for girls.
This module discusses the consequences of child marriage on girls' educational opportunities.

* Gain insights into the global issue of child marriage and its widespread impact on young girls.
* Explore the root causes of child marriage and understand the factors contributing to this harmful practice.
* Learn how child marriage frequently leads to the interruption of girls' education and its far-reaching implications.
* Examine the consequences of child marriage on girls' educational opportunities, including long-term effects.
* Develop a comprehensive understanding of the multifaceted challenges posed by child marriage and its impact on the lives of young girls.
