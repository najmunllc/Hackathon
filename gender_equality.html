Empowering Women Through Financial Literacy and Entrepreneurship

Description: Our "Gender Bias" course addresses the critical issue of gender
inequality and discrimination that persists in society.
This course aims to raise awareness and empower individuals to
challenge and change biased attitudes and behaviors. It provides valuable insights into understanding,
identifying, and combatting gender bias in various aspects of life, from the workplace to everyday interactions

* Increase awareness about the prevalence of gender bias.
* Participants will learn how to recognize instances of gender bias in their personal and professional lives.
* It empowers individuals to be agents of change, advocating for gender equality in their communities
* Overcome economic challenges and build a successful business.
* Empower yourself through financial literacy and entrepreneurship training.
