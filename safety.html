Personal Safety and Empowerment

Description: This course is designed to empower women with the knowledge and skills necessary
to enhance their personal safety, including self-defense techniques.
Participants will learn strategies to recognize and avoid potential threats,
as well as physical self-defense techniques. The course emphasizes empowerment through preparedness and confidence.

* Enhance Personal Safety Awareness
* Develop Self-Defense Skills
* Promote Online Safety
* Build a Support Network
* Building Confidence
